About Me

Hello! I am Safuan Iusupov, a Computer Vision & Machine Learning Engineer with 2.5+ years of experience leading research and applied projects from concept to deployment. I specialize in building end-to-end ML pipelines for Computer Vision, with a focus on 3D Vision (NeRF, 3D Gaussian Splatting), anomaly detection, and object detection. Proficient in deep learning, classical CV, and model optimization, I'm passionate about education and have lectured at MIPT & Yandex. I enjoy exploring the intersection of CV, DL, and LLMs to create more functional and intelligent systems.

Currently pursuing a Master's degree in the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master in Imaging, having completed my first year at Polytechnic di Milano (Polimi) and now in my second year at Mid Sweden University.
